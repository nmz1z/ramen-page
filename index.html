RECIPES

Ramen

Flavored with pork and chicken broth with a mix of toppings such as chashu and ramen egg, this bowl of Miso Ramen is going to satisfy your craving.

Katsudon

Crispy pork cutlet and runny egg simmered in a savory broth and served on a bed of hot steamed rice.

Ebi Fry

Ebi Fry is a Japanese fried shrimp made with succulent shrimp coated with Panko breadcrumbs and fried till golden brown.

Takoyaki

Takoyaki, or Grilled Octopus Balls, originated in Osaka and are one of Japan’s best-known street foods.
